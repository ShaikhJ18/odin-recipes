Lasagna

Description

This recipe is a delicious blend of meat, cheese, and pasta sheets.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Ground meat
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. boil the Pasta sheets
  2. Cook the Ground meat and season it too
  3. Assemble the layers of the lasagna
  4. put a layer of lasagna sheet in the tray
  5. Add Tomato sauce on top of the sheet
  6. Add Ground meat on top
  7. Add cheese on the top of that
  8. repeat this layering process until the tray is full
  9. Finally put the tray in the oven
  10. wait until the whole thing is well cooked
