Chocolate Milkshake

Description

This recipe is a delicious blend of milk and chocolate.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • chocolate mix
  • Sugar
  • Whipped cream

Steps

  1. Take some milk and pour it into a blender
  2. put a couple of scoops of chocolate mix into the blender
  3. turn on the blender and run it for 2 mins
  4. mix some sugar into the blender
  5. run the blender again
  6. pour the mixture into a glass
  7. Add some whipped cream on top
  8. Enjoy your drink
