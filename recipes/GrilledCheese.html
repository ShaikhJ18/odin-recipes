GrilledCheese

Description

This recipe is a delicious sandwich consisting of cheese and well cooked toast.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Bread
  • Seasoning
  • Tomato sauce
  • butter

Steps

  1. take out your bread
  2. butter your bread
  3. toast your bread in a toaster or pan
  4. fill your toast with tomato sauce and cheese
  5. Season your sandwich
  6. toast your sandwich one more time
  7. plate your sandiwch and enjoy your meal
